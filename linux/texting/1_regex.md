Regular Expressions
============

**Match what ?**

`.`  
`[kl]` any one of the characters enclosed in [chars]  
`(tri)` treats the terms enclosed as one entity  
`[^01]`   
`[1-5]`

**Match how many times ?**

`?`
`+`
`*`
`{n}`
`{n,}`
`{n, m}`

**Others** 

`\\`  
`|`  
`^` the start of the line marker  
`$` the end of the line marker
